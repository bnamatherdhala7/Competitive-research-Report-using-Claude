02

Adobe's Real Position

Where we win that we're not telling anyone — and the gaps that are costing us a $5B+ addressable market.

Where Adobe Wins

Unlimited free — for real. No credit cap, no watermark, no account required for basic use. remove.bg gives you 1 low-res preview per day. Canva caps at 10/month then charges $120/year.
Best quality on hard images. Transparent objects, glass, smoke, fine fabric — Adobe tops every benchmark. remove.bg wins on hair; Adobe wins everywhere else.
Full workflow, not one step. Remove → AI replace background → design → export — all in Express. No competitor offers this end-to-end without stitching three tools together.
Generative Fill is unmatched. Photoshop's Generative Fill for background replacement has no direct competitor. remove.bg, PhotoRoom, Claid can only remove — Adobe can remove and rebuild with AI.
Commercial safety. Every background-removed asset is IP-clean with full indemnification. For brands and enterprises, this is a legal requirement — not a checkbox.

Where We're Losing

Rank #6 for the exact keyword. remove.bg's domain name is the search query. Adobe's free unlimited tool is invisible to the 18M people searching each month.
No standalone API product. Every developer building an e-commerce tool or app reaches for remove.bg or PhotoRoom's API. Adobe has no self-serve, per-image API for this use case.
E-commerce segment is a blind spot. 10M+ Shopify sellers use PhotoRoom, Claid.ai, and remove.bg for daily catalog work. They don't know Adobe has a free, better alternative.
No batch processing in Express. E-commerce sellers need 100+ images at once — this is why they pay Claid $59/1,000. Adobe requires uploading one at a time.
CC framing buries the feature. Remove background is positioned as a CC subscriber benefit — not surfaced as a free standalone utility that happens to have an upgrade path.
